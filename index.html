History of Cultivation of plants

  • 8000-2500 BC Wheat is the first cereal to be cultivated by man. In several places in the Middle East it is being sowed, tended and reaped soon after 8000 BC.
  • Rice is thought to have been cultivated considerably later, perhaps not until about 2500 BC.

Cultivation is the act of caring for or raising plants. Your desire to grow your own fruits and vegetables in the backyard means you'll be engaged in some heavy cultivation. The word cultivation is most often used to talk about the ways that farmers take care of crops. --By Nature center
